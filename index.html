Health Risks By Demographics

Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

The key measures are: Health Care, Smoking Habits, Obesity, Age, Household Income and Poverty Rate for each state.

There are correlations of Healthcare, Obesity, Smokes and Income. For instance:

  • Mississippi (MS): has high obesity (35.5%), poverty rate (21.5%) and lowest total household income ($39,680)
  • West Virginia (WV): has highest smoking rate (26.7%), second highest obesity rate (35.7%) but the total household income is arount $41,059
  • Majority states with median age range between 36 to 42 years old, that generally are having high obesity, high smoking rates but low healthcare
